5 SOS Bio

5 Seconds of Summer are an Australian pop rock band from Sydney, New South Wales. They formed in 2011. They were first signed to Hi or Hey Records on January 27th 2014. Band mates include Luke Hemmings, Calum Hood, Michael Clifford, and Ashton Irwin.
